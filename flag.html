Why the flag?

What is the flag?

The pansexual flag is a pink, yellow and cyan flag, designed in 2010 as a symbol for the pansexual community to increase its visibility and recognition, like the rainbow flag is for the LGBT community. It consists of three equally-sized horizontal bars, colored pink, yellow, and cyan from top to bottom.

Why do you show it?

I identify as pansexual, as gender plays little role in whether I am attracted to other people sexually or romantically. I am attracted to people regardless of their gender identity, gender expression, or biological sex. I respect that most folks have preferences. I support everyone in their identities of who they're born to love.
